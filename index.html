Our Flagship Product

Meet Smart Janja

A software solution designed specifically for small and medium-sized businesses. Smart Janja is built with simplicity in mind, making it easy to use even for users with little or no technical experience.

Record Sales

Easily record daily sales with a user-friendly interface designed for local shop owners.

Manage Stock

Automatically manage your inventory and know exactly what you have in stock at all times.

Instant Reports

Generate comprehensive business reports instantly to understand your financial health.

Track Performance

Track business performance in real time to make informed decisions and increase profitability.
